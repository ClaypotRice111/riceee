Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Riceee AI?

Riceee AI is a lightweight, private AI assistant for Windows that runs entirely on your PC using your local LM Studio model. No data leaves your computer. No cloud. No tracking.

2. How does it work?

Riceee AI connects to your local LM Studio server (running on http://localhost:1865). It sends your voice/text input to your model and returns the response — all locally. You control the AI.

3. Do I need an internet connection?

Only for the first-time setup and model downloads. After that, you can use Riceee AI completely offline. Your data never goes to the cloud.

4. What models work with Riceee AI?

Any model supported by LM Studio: Mistral, Phi-3, TinyLlama, Llama 3, Qwen, etc. We recommend models under 4GB for best performance on average PCs.

5. Is it safe? Does it collect my data?

Absolutely. Riceee AI never sends your data to any server. All processing happens locally. We don’t log anything. You’re in full control.

6. Can I use it with my own model?

Yes! Just load your preferred model in LM Studio, start the server, and Riceee AI will connect automatically. No configuration needed.

7. Why not use a web-based AI?

Web-based AIs send your data to remote servers — they can store, analyze, or sell it. Riceee AI keeps everything private. Your privacy is the default.

8. What if LM Studio isn’t running?

Riceee AI will show a connection error. Make sure LM Studio is open and the server is running on port 1865. You’ll see a green status when connected.

9. Is it free?

Yes! The core app is free. We may offer optional premium features in the future (e.g. voice enhancement, custom themes), but the core AI experience will always remain free.

10. How do I report a bug?

Email us at support@riceee.com. Include your OS version, LM Studio version, and a description of the issue.
